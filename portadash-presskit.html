App Summary

PortaDash is a free, ad-free, community-powered iOS app that helps people find the nearest public restroom or porta potty instantly. Built for runners, parents, travelers, and commuters, PortaDash eliminates one of daily life's most universal stress points with a clean, fast, location-aware interface.

The app is free forever, contains no ads, and is powered by community-contributed locations — meaning every user makes the map better for everyone else. Available on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Vision. Requires iOS 18.6 or later.

Why It Matters

  • Finding a restroom quickly is a daily pain point for millions of people worldwide
  • Especially urgent for runners, parents with young children, people with health conditions, tourists, and commuters
  • Most mapping apps don't prioritize or filter restrooms and porta potties effectively
  • PortaDash is purpose-built for this one need — fast, clean, focused

About the Founder

Founder Bio

LS

Lance Song

Founder, PortaDash · Nanuda Labs LLC

Lance Song is a Richmond, Virginia-based creator and entrepreneur who built PortaDash to solve a real-world problem he and his workout group encountered constantly: finding a nearby restroom quickly when you're mid-run, out with kids, or exploring a new city.

PortaDash was developed as a community-first utility — free, ad-free, and designed to get better with every person who uses it. The app earned early media coverage in Richmond and has been organically shared across workout and running communities.

Lance believes the best apps solve one specific problem exceptionally well, and stay out of the way otherwise.
